# APPLICATION CONTEXT

## Water Conservation Challenge in Mozambique

### Current Situation:
- **Water scarcity**: 52% of population lacks reliable water access
- **Infrastructure gaps**: Aging pipes cause significant water loss
- **Economic impact**: Water damage costs millions annually
- **Climate vulnerability**: Increased flooding risks

### Why This System Matters:
1. **Early detection** prevents catastrophic water damage
2. **Data collection** enables preventive maintenance
3. **Cost-effective** solution for widespread deployment
4. **Educational tool** for water conservation awareness
