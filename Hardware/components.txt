=== WATER LEAK DETECTION SYSTEM - COMPONENTS LIST ===

1. Arduino Uno R3 (or compatible)
2. Water Level Sensor (conductive type)
3. Tilt Switch / Vibration Sensor
4. HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor (optional)
5. Active Buzzer
6. RGB LED
7. Resistors: 3x 220Ω, 1x 10kΩ
8. Breadboard and jumper wires
9. USB cable for power

Total Cost: ~$25 USD
Purpose: Early detection of water leaks in homes/industries
Application: Mozambique residential and commercial use
